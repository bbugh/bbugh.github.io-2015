About Me

I grew up below the US poverty line and technology helped me get out of the dead end life I would have had. I reached a level of success I was happy with, and then realized I wasn't all that happy anymore just filling my bank account. I've spent the last few years figuring out how I can best pay my gifts forward, and teaching/mentoring has been the experiment that has worked the best.

My mission is to inspire, mentor, and educate others to their own greatness through technology & philanthropy
